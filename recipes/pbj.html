PBJ Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of sliced bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly or jam

Steps

  1. Slather peanut butter on one side of the first slice of bread.
  2. Slather your jam or jelly onto one side of the other slice of bread.
  3. Place the jelly side of the second slice onto the peanut butter side of the first, lining the slices up to overlap as much as possible.
